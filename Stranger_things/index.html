Welcome to Hawkins

On November 6, 1983,
Will Byers went missing in Hawkins, Indiana.
Long after the town gave up the search,
His friends kept looking...
And discovered a mysterious girl : Eleven.
She was alone, hunted, and extremely powerful.
Eleven would stop at nothing to help them find their friend.
Together, they would turn the world upside down.
